# Cap'n Blox's Ottoman Empire

## 🚢 Overview

**Cap'n Blox's Ottoman Empire** is an immersive psychological horror and exploration experience on Roblox heavily inspired by *The Backrooms* and the A24 Backrooms film. Blending unsettling liminal spaces with a nautical theme, players navigate unique, maze-like levels filled with mysterious finds, rare decor gems, and hidden secrets.

## ✨ Key Features

* Cover eerie, retro-themed multi-level environments inspired by classic Backrooms lore.
* Experience a unique blend of nautical folklore and liminal architecture designed for high graphics and immersive sound.

## 🖥️ Platform Compatibility

Optimized for play across multiple platforms:

* PC
* Mobile
* Console
